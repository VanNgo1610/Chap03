Egg Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 3 teaspoons of Vietnamese coffee powder
  • 2 teaspoons of sweetened condensed milk
  • Boiling water

How to make

  1. Brew a small cup of Vietnamese coffee.
  2. Crack an egg and separate the yolk from the whites. Discard the whites.
  3. Put the yolk and the sweetened condensed milk in a small, deep bowl and whisk vigorously until you end up with a frothy, fluffy mixture like the one above
  4. Add a tablespoon of the brewed coffee and whisk it in.
  5. In a clear coffee cup, pour in your brewed coffee, then add the fluffy egg mixture on top.
  6. Presto. Egg coffee.
